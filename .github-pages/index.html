Obiddable

The modern open source bidding system for Pennsylvania school districts.

Streamlined, Clear, and Built for Real Workflows

Obiddable delivers a structured process for collecting district requests, generating vendor bid packages, handling returned pricing sheets, electing winners based on cost and suitability, and producing clean purchase orders.

Key Capabilities

Item Catalog Management

Easily maintain a consistent, centralized list of items for all district requests.

Requestor Input Tools

Auto generated spreadsheets let requestors enter needs cleanly and import them back with no friction.

Vendor Bid Packages

Create vendor facing sheets that collect pricing and availability in a predictable format.

Bid Elections

Evaluate vendor responses by cost and suitability to determine the most practical choices.

Purchase Orders

Finalize the workflow by generating clean, ready to issue purchase orders.

Why It Stands Out

Obiddable avoids guesswork by using structured spreadsheets at every step. District staff and vendors work with clear, reliable templates backed by consistent validation.
