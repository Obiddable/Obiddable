Simple Workflow

From items to purchase orders in a streamlined process

01

Create or Roll Bid

Start fresh or roll from a previous bid, automatically importing items, requestors, and historical pricing data.

02

Manage Items & Requestors

Configure your item catalog and set up requestors with their account codes. Mass update pricing as needed.

03

Collect Requests

Generate password-protected Excel sheets for each requestor. They fill them out and send them back for easy import.

04

Send to Vendors

Create vendor specification sheets and blank response Excel files to distribute to your vendors.

05

Evaluate & Elect

Run automated low-bid processing, manually adjust elections based on needs, and generate comprehensive reports.

06

Generate Purchase Orders

Auto-create purchase orders organized by vendor and building, ready for Tyler ERP import.
